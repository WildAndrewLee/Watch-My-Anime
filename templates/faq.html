Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not getting email notifications?

There are several reasons as to why you may not be receiving email notifications.

  • Email from Watch My Anime may be marked as spam by your email provider.
  • Watch My Anime uses Mandrill to send emails. Sometimes emails sent through Mandrill may take a few extra minutes to actually be sent.
  • Watch My Anime may not be able to find new releases for your anime even though they are on Nyaa. In this case you should make sure you entered the anime's information correctly. In addition, Watch My Anime utilizes sub group naming conventions when searching for releases. This means that if a torrent name does not match something such as [Sub Group] Anime Name - Episode [Hash][Quality][Hash].ext, then it will not be recognized as a release by Watch My Anime.

How often does Watch my Anime check for new releases?

Watch My Anime currently checks for new releases at one minute intervals.

How do I unwatch an anime?

To unwatch an anime you can use the link provided in each email notification.

Will Watch My Anime be free forever?

Watch My Anime will always be free.

How does Watch My Anime check for new episodes?

Watch My Anime uses Nyaa's RSS feed in order to pull information about new releases.

Is Watch My Anime open source?

Watch My Anime is, and always will be, open source. The source code is currently hosted on Github.
